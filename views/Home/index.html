Our School

Read More


At Kinderschool, we have a simple yet beautiful motto: ''be brave!' In school we talk about how being brave or intrepid takes many forms. Being brave isn’t always a grand gesture; sometimes it just means ‘having a go’; attempting that difficult question, offering an answer in a lesson when you’re not quite sure - or simply trying something new.
We offer a safe, nurturing and stimulating environment in which this culture of intrepidness can flourish, allowing Kinderschool children to be brave and adventurous as they learn and grow

Our Philosophy

Kinderschool believes children are born eager and ready to learn! 
As educators, we foster  learning by providing them with opportunities that allow them to add their own meaning to the world around them. 
We understand that not only is it important for children to develop the basic skills foundational for future learning, but also that they receive care from teachers who have a genuine love for children.

Our Goal

We strive to provide exceptional care, a safe and nurturing environment, creating an enriching learning experience for each child 
We seek out the most compassionate and qualified staff to care for your child.
At Kinderschool Preschool the goals for children in our program are for them to:

  • Become active and engaged learners
  • Be safe & secure
  • Develop positive relationships, self-confidence and independence.
  • Appreciate cultural diversity
  • Have fun while learning!
