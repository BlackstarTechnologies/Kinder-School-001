Welcome

At Kinderschool, we have a simple motto: ''be brave!' 
We teach how being brave can take many forms. 

Being brave isn’t always a grand gesture
It can also mean "just having a go"
attempting a difficult question, offering an answer in a lesson when you’re not quite sure - or simply trying something new

We offer a safe, nurturing and stimulating environment in which this culture of intrepidness can flourish, allowing Kinderschool children to be brave and adventurous as they learn and grow

At Kinderschool, our priority is  our pupils. 
We endeavour to create a caring, supportive environment that enables each individual to develop and shine.

Nurturing and engaging

Established in 2020 by a former preschool teacher herself, Esther Kabura, have cultivated a learning environment where children are supported, nurtured, and encouraged to be curious.
Each of our classes has a lead teacher who is certified by Ministry Of Education and also holds a  Diploma in Early Childhood Education. Every class also has qualified assistant teachers with a similar background in childhood education.

2/3 Classroom
(2-3 years)

Your younger preschooler will build on basic concepts such as counting, letter and number recognition and phonemic awareness while having plenty of opportunities to play and improve his/her social skills.

Toddler Classroom
(15-24 months)

With our low teacher to child ratio, we are able to provide a high level of teacher interaction with your child. Your toddler will explore, question and discover with age appropriate materials and activities.

Preschool Classroom
(4-5 years)

Our preschool program prepares your child for the next exciting chapter in his/her educational career…
kindergarten!

We use a combination of teacher-guided small and whole group learning experiences, child-guided activities and free choice centers...

We help your child strengthen their  social and emotional skills, 
physical, language and literacy abilities, mathematical reasoning,  science and creative interests.

Beyond the classroom

We feel it is important for children to see that learning can occur outside of their regular classroom. Fieldtrips and other extracurricular activities will compliment a particular theme that is being discussed in the classroom. Our goal is for all fieldtrips and extracurricular activities to be educational and fun.

Outdoor play

We have the privilege of using the beautiful spacious compound for our outdoor play. Children have plenty of room to run and play and exert all that energy their little bodies build up throughout the day.

Daily reports

A daily report will be provided so that parents can see all the activities in which their child participated, along with other events that went on during that particular day.

Enrichment classes

Kinderschool Preschool offers Music, dance and art classes that meet once at week at our center.
